About Me

I'm a front end web developer who loves building accessible, user friendly websites, crafting, and birds. I can be found learning about the lastest web technologies, cycling along the seafront, or singing with The Dulcetones. I was touched by a humboldt penguin at Artis, Amsterdam, and have been elated ever since.

I graduated from the University of Sussex with a Masters degree in Computing with Digital Media, and for the past 4 years have been developing websites in Brighton. I currently work for MBA, one of the world's leading independent agencies.
